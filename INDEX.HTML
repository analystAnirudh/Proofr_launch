About Proofr

Founded by SRCC students, we're revolutionizing early-career hiring by replacing traditional resumes with proof-of-work

Bridging the Gap Between Academia and Industry

Proofr is a pre-seed startup by SRCC undergraduates revolutionizing early-career hiring. We replace traditional resumes with proof-of-work, enabling students to showcase real project experience.

Through curated job boards, portfolio tools, and cohort-based upskilling programs, Proofr connects talent with companies seeking demonstrated skill over pedigree.

Our initiatives include expert-led sessions covering CV building, finance technicals, case solving, and more, conducted by alums from top firms like MBBs and Avendus.

Placements 101 Fellowship

A 21-day, hands-on program designed for people serious about landing internships and placements

Week 1

Placement Essentials

  • Craft a placement-ready CV
  • Answer behavioral questions confidently
  • Build technical foundations
  • Prepare for first-round interviews
  • Understand recruiter expectations

Week 2

Domain Deep Dive

  • Understand what top firms look for
  • Solve cases like a consultant
  • Model like a finance analyst
  • Think like a product manager
  • Build role-specific strengths

Week 3

Fellowship Sprint

  • Apply learning in real-world simulation
  • Complete guided domain-specific project
  • Receive certificate of completion
  • Get referrals & LinkedIn endorsements
  • Showcase industry-ready output
